The Blog of Tcal-X

Why "tcal-x" ? Well, my work login "tcal" was already taken at GitHub. When I thought of "x" as an arbitrary extension, "tcal-x" stuck in my head because it combined "tcal" (of course) and "kalx" -- the UC Berkeley student-run radio station. KALX had introduced me to the Smashing Pumpkins, Massive Attack, and maybe Green Day (I can't remember I first heard Green Day on KALX, or live on Lower Sproul Plaza....).
